About us

Tura Day Secondary School provides quality education and holistic development for learners.

At Tura day Secondary school, we pride ourselves on:
Academic excellence through well structured programs
promoting care values such as integrity, respect and perseverance
Offering wide range of extra-curricular activities including sports, arts and clubs
Providing modern facilities that support holistic development, including sports fields dedicated and experienced staff,
our teachers are passionate, qualified and committed to student success
Innovative learning environment, We intergrate technology and modern teaching methods in classrooms
